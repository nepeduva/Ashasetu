Our Mission 2026

AshaSetu is a dedicated friends-led initiative focused on serving our brothers and sisters living on the streets. Our goal is simple: providing basic nutrition and a sense of belonging to those forgotten by the world.

We don't just deliver food; we deliver hope. By focusing on practical, non-perishable kits, we ensure that help is immediate and effective, reaching those in the darkest corners of our cities.

INITIATIVE LEAD

Abdul Subhan

Guided by the vision of Abdul Subhan, AshaSetu operates on the ground with zero bureaucracy. Abdul believes in direct action—personally overseeing the distribution of kits to ensure every contribution translates into a smile on the street.

"We don't need a massive organization to make a massive difference. We just need to care."

The 2026 Impact

The Support Strategy

We provide a "Dignity Kit" carefully designed for immediate relief and energy:

  • Variety of Snacks
  • Fresh Water Bottle
  • High-nutrition Chikki
  • Gift of some money for essentials

Strategic Reach

Our 2026 roadmap focuses on "Grey Zones"—areas often missed by large NGOs:

  • Railway station outskirts
  • Construction site clusters
  • Under-bridge communities
  • Night-shelter waitlists

Radical Transparency

Every contribution is documented and utilized immediately:

  • 100% funds go to procurement
  • Zero administrative deductions
  • Volunteer-powered logistics
  • Visual proof of distribution

Goal for 2026:To help 10,000+ people across the region, creating a consistent "Bridge of Hope" through weekend distribution drives.

Clear & Honest

Status: AshaSetu is a personal group of friends. We are not a registered NGO. We operate purely on shared trust and a common goal of helping our fellow humans in India.

Note: This initiative does not promise outcomes and operates on a best-effort basis.
